After the first 8 looks:

- **Mondays and Thursdays only.** 2 posts per week.

- Rotate content types:

### Content types (rotate)

**Garment detail** — close crop of a single piece. Caption: fabric weight + one line.
> "Bone oxford. Japanese 3.5oz cotton. Single-needle topstitch at the collar."

**Typographic posts** — Fraunces pull-quote from Antecedents on bone ground. 1080×1080, ink type, no ornament.
> Quote the Vest page, the Hoodie page, the Turtleneck page. One per post.

**Texture** — SF or Kyoto environmental shots that share the site's palette. Never with people. Caption: place + one line.
> "Kyoto. The courtyard behind the atelier."

**Correspondence (anonymized)** — reader letters, redacted, set as typographic posts. Powerful when real; don't fake.
> "'I bought nothing and read everything. — a reader in Helsinki.'"

**Process** — scanned pattern pieces, hand-sketched production notes. One per month at most. Never full shots of workrooms.

### What you don't post, ever

- ❌ Reels
- ❌ Stories (turn them off — do not cross the line into ephemeral)
- ❌ Carousels > 3 images
- ❌ BTS / "meet the founder" content
- ❌ Announcement posts
- ❌ "Link in bio 👇"
- ❌ Pricing, "shop now," buttons
- ❌ Press-coverage reposts even if Spyplane posts about you
- ❌ User-generated content reposts (there are no users)
- ❌ Engagement bait ("which look?")
- ❌ Polls / quiz stickers
- ❌ Carousel with text slides

### DMs

## One call to make before launch

**Personal IG vs. new brand IG.** If you have a personal IG with any following, consider cross-promoting once (one post saying "I made this," linking to @hayes.press) — a single controlled bridge from your personal graph. This is the *one* exception to "don't announce."

Risk: once the personal announcement happens, the accounts are linked in people's heads forever. HAYES is less fun if everyone knows PJ-from-SV runs it. If you'd rather preserve the slight mystery, skip the cross-post entirely and let HAYES find its own audience.

**Recommendation:** skip. The mystery is part of the voice. Your close friends will find it anyway.
