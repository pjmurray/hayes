# HAYES — Network Tracker

> One place to track who received what, when, and how they reacted.
> Canonical source for "have I heard back?"
> Companion to `TIER1.md` (editorial context + email drafts) and `MAIL.md` (addresses + ship day).

---

## Rules of use

- **Follow silently** from your personal accounts this week. No likes, comments, replies, DMs until a card/email has been sent.
- **Don't LinkedIn** any of these people. Wrong register.
- **Fill "Obsession" within 3-5 days of following** — what they're posting/writing about right now. This is your opener when you eventually meet.
- **Flag mutuals with ⚡** as you identify them. ⚡ rows should get a DM-the-friend check *before* the cold send.
- **Update Sent/Response after every touchpoint.** This file is the source of truth.
- **No follow-ups, ever** — if they go silent, mark "silent 60d" and move on.
- **[verify]** flags mean I'm not 100% on the handle — click to confirm before following.

## Legend

- **Channel**: Mail = physical card to office address. Email = personal-Gmail send Apr 23. DM = Twitter/IG/Farcaster. "Mail + Email" = both.
- **Sent**: date you shipped / sent the email / sent the DM.
- **Obsession**: single-line summary of what they're currently posting/writing about. Updated continuously. This is your opener.
- **Mutuals**: known shared connections. ⚡ = DM the mutual before sending.
- **Response**: date + nature (reply / like / quote / silence). "silent 60d" = resolved no-response.

---

## Handle-verification process (~15 min, do once)

Open each [verify]-flagged link before following. Correct any wrong handles in this file, then commit. A wrong follow is worse than no follow — means you're not who you say you are in their "recent followers" list.

Quick way to verify: Google `"[name]" twitter` or `"[name]" instagram` — first result is usually right. Cross-check: the profile should have recent activity and a photo that matches.

---

## Update protocol

After each touchpoint:

1. Fill **Sent** with the date.
2. If a response arrives, fill **Response** same-day.
3. If no response after 60 days, mark `silent 60d` — that row is resolved.
4. If a public post / like / quote comes in, note it in Response and **like back only from @hayes.press IG**. Never reply from either account unless they DM directly.

Commit this file after each update — it's your ledger.
